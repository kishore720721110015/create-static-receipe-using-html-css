Grandmas's Secret Coffee

This coffee receipe has been in my family for generation and never fails to delight.



Ingredients

  • Instant coffee- 2 tbsp
  • Sugar- 2tpsp
  • Hot water- 2tpsp
  • Milk- 170ml


Instructions

  1. Boil the water
  2. Warm up your mug
  3. Add your favourite instant coffee to your mug
  4. Add water and give it a good stir
  5. Add milk and sugar, if you like
  6. Make it fancy!
